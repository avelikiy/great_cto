# Brief №2 — Client portal (catalog №6, A1 CRUD)

> Frozen 2026-07-10 · Feed to `/start` verbatim · Do not edit after the batch starts.

A client portal for a 12-person marketing agency. Each client company gets logins to
see their projects' status, deliverables waiting for their approval, and their invoice
history in one place — replacing the current mess of email threads. Agency staff
upload deliverables, request approval, and see exactly who approved what and when;
approval must be one click from an email link, with a full audit trail. Clients can
comment on a deliverable to request changes, which flips it back to in-revision.
Integrations: Stripe for paying invoices from the portal, email notifications for
approval requests and comments.
