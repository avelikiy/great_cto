# Brief №3 — Applicant tracking system (catalog №29, A1 CRUD) · wave 0

> Frozen 2026-07-10 · Feed to `/start` verbatim · Do not edit after the batch starts.

An applicant tracking system for a 50-person company that hires about 10 roles a
year. Recruiters post roles; candidates apply through a public form with resume
upload; applications land in a per-role pipeline (screen → interview → offer →
hired / rejected) that recruiters manage by drag or click. Hiring managers score
candidates against a structured rubric and leave feedback that's visible to the
hiring team but never to the candidate. Recruiters schedule interviews from inside
the app and the system emails invites, reminders, and rejection notes from
templates. Integrations: Google Calendar for interview scheduling, email for all
candidate communication.
