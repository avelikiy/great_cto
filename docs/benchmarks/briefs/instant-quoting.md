# Brief №8 — Instant quoting / estimate builder (catalog №2, A5 Marketplace-lite)

> Frozen 2026-07-10 · Feed to `/start` verbatim · Do not edit after the batch starts.
> Flagged in the plan as an *expected-hard* case — published either way.

An instant quoting tool for a fencing contractor. A homeowner enters fence type,
linear feet, number of gates, and terrain difficulty (with optional photo uploads),
and gets a priced quote range in under a minute — computed from the contractor's own
price book of materials, labor rates, and margins, which the contractor maintains in
an admin screen. The contractor reviews each auto-generated quote, adjusts line
items, and sends a final version the customer can accept and e-sign online. An
accepted quote converts to a job with a deposit invoice attached. Integrations:
Stripe for deposits, e-signature on the final quote.
