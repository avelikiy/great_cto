# Brief №1 — Dispatch & scheduling app (catalog №1, A1 CRUD)

> Frozen 2026-07-10 · Feed to `/start` verbatim · Do not edit after the batch starts.

A dispatch and scheduling app for a small HVAC and plumbing company — 8 field
technicians, 2 dispatchers. Dispatchers create jobs from phone calls, assign them to
technicians by skill and availability, and see the whole day on a job board with live
status. Technicians open the app on their phone to see their assigned jobs in order,
mark themselves en-route / started / done, and attach notes and photos to each job.
The office needs a searchable customer history per service address and an end-of-day
summary of completed vs. slipped jobs. Integrations: Twilio SMS to text the customer
"your tech is on the way" when a job goes en-route.
