# Brief №5 — Class booking + membership (catalog №21, A2 Booking)

> Frozen 2026-07-10 · Feed to `/start` verbatim · Do not edit after the batch starts.

A class booking and membership app for a yoga studio with 2 rooms and about 15
classes a week. Members buy a membership tier (unlimited / 8-a-month) or a class
pack, book classes with per-class capacity limits, and join a waitlist that
auto-promotes when someone cancels inside the policy window. Front desk sees a
roster per class and checks members in with one tap; no-shows are tracked against
the member. Memberships renew and bill automatically; a failed card triggers a
dunning email sequence before access is paused. Integrations: Stripe Billing for
memberships and packs, email for waitlist promotions and dunning.
