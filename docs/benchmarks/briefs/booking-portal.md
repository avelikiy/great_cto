# Brief №4 — Customer booking portal (catalog №3, A2 Booking)

> Frozen 2026-07-10 · Feed to `/start` verbatim · Do not edit after the batch starts.

A customer booking portal for a mobile car-detailing business with 3 crews.
Customers pick a service package, see real availability that accounts for crew
schedules and drive-time buffers between jobs, book a slot, and pay a deposit
online. The owner sets working hours per crew, blocks out days off, and starts each
morning from a clean daily schedule view. The system sends an SMS confirmation at
booking, a reminder 24 hours before, and a "book your next detail" link after the
job is done; customers can reschedule from a link without calling. Integrations:
Stripe for deposits, Twilio for all SMS.
