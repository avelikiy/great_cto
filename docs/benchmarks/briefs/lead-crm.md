# Brief №6 — Lead CRM + nurture (catalog №18, A3 CRM)

> Frozen 2026-07-10 · Feed to `/start` verbatim · Do not edit after the batch starts.

A lead CRM with automated nurture for a 4-agent residential real-estate team. Leads
arrive from the team's website form or manual entry, get auto-assigned to an agent
round-robin, and move through a pipeline: new → contacted → touring → offer →
closed. Every stage has an automated email/SMS nurture sequence that pauses the
moment an agent logs a real conversation, and resumes if the lead goes quiet for a
week. Agents start the day from a follow-ups-due view; the team lead sees conversion
rates by lead source to decide where the marketing budget goes. Integrations: Twilio
for SMS, email for sequences.
