# Brief №10 — Coaching content delivery (catalog №22, A6 Content)

> Frozen 2026-07-10 · Feed to `/start` verbatim · Do not edit after the batch starts.

A coaching content platform for an online fitness coach selling three program tiers.
The coach uploads video lessons and PDF plans into structured programs (weeks → days
→ items); clients get access according to their tier, work through the program,
mark workouts complete, and log daily habit check-ins. Clients see their streaks and
completion progress; the coach sees who's falling behind and can message them a
nudge from inside the app. Content is gated — video streams are not downloadable and
share links don't work for non-subscribers. Integrations: Stripe subscriptions for
the three tiers, email for weekly progress digests.
