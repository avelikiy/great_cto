# Brief №9 — Subcontractor coordination portal (catalog №35, A5 Marketplace-lite)

> Frozen 2026-07-10 · Feed to `/start` verbatim · Do not edit after the batch starts.

A subcontractor coordination portal for a general contractor running 5–10 active
jobs at a time. The GC posts scoped work packages (drywall, electrical, roofing)
with plan documents and deadlines; invited subs view the scope, ask questions in a
per-package thread, and submit bids. The GC compares bids side by side, awards the
package, and from then on tracks that sub's milestones, change orders, and payment
status per job. Subs log in and see only their own packages and documents — never
another sub's bid or another job. Integrations: email notifications for invites,
questions, and awards; document upload for plans and change orders.
